Every Thursday (Term time only)

TTaB - Social Club is every Thursday 11AM to 3PM at The Exchange, Watkinson road, N7 8DE.

Itenerary

  • 11AM - 1PM: IT Support
  • 1PM - 2PM: Chair exercise
  • 2PM - 3PM: Lunch

Learn how to use a computer, apps, the web and more!

We provide a short presentation on key skills, with additional support available from our friendly staff of Trainee Junior IT Support Technicians.

Fun Chair Exercises for Strength, Balance, and Fitness

Join us for fun, engaging chair exercises designed to help you feel fit and strong.
Simple moves, big benefits and no standing required!
Perfect for all levels, come move, stretch, and feel great.

Lunch, served fresh by Better Together Kitchen

End the day off with a scrumptious lunch cooked fresh every Thursday by our top class chef, Meshach.

General Pricing:
  • Main: £3.50
  • Dessert: £2

Older Residents:
  • Main: Free
  • Dessert: £1
